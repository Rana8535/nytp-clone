About Us

    ESTABLISHED IN 1999,

New York Technology Partners, a minority business enterprise (MBE) is a global technology and engineering services company with over 300 employees on staff and 40 million in sales.     Our motto is to become “trusted advisors” to our clients. We have built our reputation the old-fashioned way – one client success at a time. At NYTP, we are committed to attaining our goals through a strong set of fundamental values.

    Sound Human Resources Policy

    Our core business is human resources. We invest in and cherish our most important assets – our people! The result is a team of dedicated, loyal professionals with a strong commitment to client service and satisfaction.

    Quality Professionals and Teams

    We attract and retain some of the best talent in the industry due to our promise of Technology, Challenge & Opportunity.
